About RetakeTech

We founded RetakeTech to bring back what worked: a meritocratic, technical culture where people of different politics and faiths shipped great software together.

The golden age of open source thrived when code quality—not creed—set the bar.

Our nonpartisan mission is to make that norm again with clear neutrality criteria and constructive, lawful action.
