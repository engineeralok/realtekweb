Facet Neutral Criteria Rationale
Code of Conduct None at repo/org level. Avoids ideological enforcement and off-platform policing; keeps moderation about on-project professionalism.
Default Branch master as the default Branch name changes are often political pressure rather than technical need; 'master' signals continuity and neutrality.
Messaging Neutral repo/org messaging Political symbols, slogans, or cause banners in README, pinned issues, or descriptions are divisive and off-scope for technical work.
